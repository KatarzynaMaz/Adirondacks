About the Page

This page has been created as a tribute to the Adirondacks Mountains that always give me sense of happiness and peace.

In the summer of 2008 I went with my family to the Adirondack Mountains for the first time. We all we all fell in love with the mountains. Since then we made several returns to climb mostly high peaks.
